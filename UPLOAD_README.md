# Escape the Boss 🏢💣

A retro Bomberman-style game where you play as an employee trying to escape from your boss!

## 🎮 Features

- 4 different boss types with unique behaviors
- 3 difficulty levels
- Power-ups: Shield, Speed Boost, Extra Bombs
- Sound effects (toggle on/off)
- Combo multiplier system
- Level progression
- Leaderboard

## 🕹️ Controls

- **Arrow Keys or WASD** - Move around
- **SPACE** - Place bomb
- Collect coins (💰) for money
- Use power-ups strategically
- Trap your boss with bomb explosions!

## 🎯 How to Play

1. Enter your boss's name
2. Choose boss type (affects movement)
3. Select difficulty
4. Click "START GAME"
5. Collect coins and avoid the boss
6. Place bombs to trap the boss
7. If boss hits you without shield = Game Over!
8. Defeat 3 levels to win!

## 🏆 Scoring

- **Coins**: $100-150 each
- **Boss Hit**: $1000 per hit (more with combos)
- **Level Complete**: $5000 bonus
- **Power-up**: $500 each

## 🎮 Game Features

### Boss Types
- 😠 **Angry Boss** - Aggressive chaser
- 😴 **Lazy Boss** - Slow, easy to avoid
- 🕵️ **Sneaky Boss** - Fast, unpredictable
- 💼 **Corporate Boss** - Strategic

### Power-ups
- 🛡️ **Shield** - Blocks one hit (5 seconds)
- ⚡ **Speed** - Double movement (8 seconds)
- 🎯 **Bombs** - Get 3 more bombs

### Levels
- Level 1: 3 HP boss, 8 coins
- Level 2: 4 HP boss, 9 coins
- Level 3: 5 HP boss, 10 coins

Good luck escaping your boss! 🚀
